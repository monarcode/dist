More About Capitalsage

Your Ultimate Gateway to Financial
Prosperity

Located in the most populous country in the most diverse continent, we have come to realise that conventional financial institutions are handicapped in creating sustainable value for the present generation and the next.

We believe that the future of banking which is here is a new world of smart, personalized and convenient customer experience. This inspired our adoption of an innovative agile business model focused on peoples profound needs and how we can create consistent financial solutions to drive transformation and unforgettable experience

Our impressive portfolio of services includes Agency Banking, Digital Savings and Investments, Value-Added Services (VAS), e-Payment Systems and Platforms, Multi-Level Networking (MLN), Point of Sales Devices(MPOS/POS) and allied services.

We possess the deep intuition and willpower to go wherever the aspirations of our customers take them and help them flourish.

Our Journey

  • 2014 - Divine Mercy

    Started as an informal business under the name Divine Mercy. The mandate was empowering market women with loans.

  • 2018 - CreditAssist Investment Limited

    Consolidated our offerings after 4 years of successful operations and business boom. On July 2nd, 2018 the business officially registered as CreditAssist Investment Limited (RC:1507157) with footprints across the country.

  • 2020 - CapitalSage Group

    Business offerings expanded and strategically evolved from a micro-finance institution into a full-pool financial technology group servicing individuals, MSMEs, corporate organisations and government institutions. By September 8, 2020, we rebranded as CapitalSage with fully integrated digital services.
